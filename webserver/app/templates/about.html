About SmartStockPredictor

SmartStockPredictor leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to provide accurate stock predictions. Our models incorporate a variety of features, including technical indicators, market sentiment, and macroeconomic factors.

Key Features:

  • Ensemble learning with Random Forests and Gradient Boosting
  • Technical analysis indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands)
  • Sentiment analysis from news and social media
  • Macroeconomic indicators (GDP growth, inflation rate)

Disclaimer:

Our predictions are based on historical data and are not guarantees of future performance. Always consult with a financial advisor before making investment decisions.
