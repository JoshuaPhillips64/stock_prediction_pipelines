How to Use Our Stock Prediction Model

Welcome to StockSage AI's advanced Stock Prediction Model Generator. Follow these simple steps to create your customized prediction:

  1. Choose your preferred model type from the dropdown menu on the right.
  2. Enter the stock symbol you want to analyze (e.g., AAPL for Apple Inc.).
  3. Select the input date for your prediction.
  4. Choose the level of hyperparameter tuning. Higher levels may improve accuracy but take longer to process.
  5. Select a feature set. Advanced features include more complex technical indicators and market sentiment data.
  6. Set the lookback period (historical data used for training) and prediction horizon (days to forecast).
  7. Click the "GENERATE" button to create your prediction model.

After generating, you'll receive a comprehensive analysis of your chosen stock, including:

  • Detailed price predictions
  • Trend indicators
  • Confidence intervals
  • Visual representations of forecast data

Note: Processing time may vary based on your selected options. More complex models and longer prediction horizons will take more time to generate.
